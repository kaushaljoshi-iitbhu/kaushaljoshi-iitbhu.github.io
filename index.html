About Me

I am a PhD scholar in Theoretical Physics at IIT (BHU), Varanasi, with research interests in quantum information theory, quantum entanglement, and many-body quantum systems. My work combines analytical understanding with numerical simulations and high-performance computing techniques.

Research Interests

  • Quantum information theory and quantum entanglement
  • Quantum thermodynamics and information–energy relations
  • Entanglement measures in quantum spin systems
  • Many-body quantum systems and quantum correlations
  • Numerical methods for strongly correlated quantum systems
  • Exact diagonalization and DMRG-based approaches
  • High-performance computing using MPI and GPU acceleration
